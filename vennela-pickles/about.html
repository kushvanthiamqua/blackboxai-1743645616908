Our Story

Traditional recipes passed down through generations, crafted with love and authentic spices

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995 by Mrs. Vennela Reddy, our pickles started as a small home kitchen operation serving just our local community in Hyderabad.

What began as sharing extra batches with neighbors quickly grew into a beloved local brand, thanks to our commitment to authentic recipes and high-quality ingredients.

Our Journey

1995 - Foundation

Started making pickles using family recipes in small batches

2002 - First Shop

Opened our first physical store in Hyderabad

2010 - State Recognition

Awarded "Best Traditional Pickles" by Telangana Food Authority

2020 - National Reach

Started shipping our pickles across India through e-commerce
